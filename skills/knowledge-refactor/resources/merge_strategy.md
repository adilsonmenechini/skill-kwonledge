# Merge Strategy

## Choose primary note

Prefer:

- Better structure
- More complete content
- Clearer explanations
- More useful examples

---

## Merge steps

- Combine unique sections
- Remove duplicated text
- Keep best phrasing
- Ensure logical flow

---

## After merge

- Update internal links if needed
- Ensure final note is self-contained

---

## Deprecation format

---
status: deprecated
---

This note has been merged into [[primary-note]].

---

## Alternative (safe mode)

If unsure:

- Do NOT merge
- Add cross-links instead
